Welcome to my personal website!

I am a PhD candidate from College of Economic and Management in China Agriculture University. My PhD project is the [Sino-Dutch Agriculture Green Development (AGD) Programme](https://www.wur.nl/en/education-programmes/phd-programme/agd-phd-program.htm), jointly organized by China Agriculture University and Wageningen Univeristy and Research. I am supervised by Prof. Dr. [Wei Si](http://cem.cau.edu.cn/art/2020/3/24/art_34695_133.html) and Prof. Dr. [Weifeng Zhang](https://zihuan.cau.edu.cn/art/2016/11/5/art_24899_481029.html) from  China Agriculture University, Prof. Dr. [Nico Heerink](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=b17mUxUAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao) from Wageningen Univeristy and Research, as well as Associate Prof. Dr. [Fan Li](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=Ns2wp84AAAAJ) from Huazhong Agriculture University. With over 5+ years of of interdisciplinary research experience spanning Economics, Sociology, and Agronomy, I specialize in agricultural policy design and evaluation. My expertise lies in applying causal inference approaches to explore viable agricultural policies for sustainable food systems. **My PhD dissertation title is titiled "From conflict to compatibility: national regulation and individual compliance in the straw-burning managemnet"**. This dissertation employ causal-inference approach, specifically the Difference-in-Differences (DID) and Spatial Regression Discontinuity (SRD) designs, to assess the extent to which and how straw-burning management in China promotes farmers' compliance with straw-burning regulations. 

In my **Job Market Paper** ([Main Text](assets/20240520_Food waste paper_Weitong Long.pdf); [Supplementary information](assets/20240520_Supplementary information_Food waste paper_Weitong Long.pdf); [Slides](assets/20240703_Food waste slides_Weitong Long.pdf)), I employed satellite-based straw burning spots to empirically assess the differential impacts of single policy instruments versus mixed policy instruments on straw burning.
